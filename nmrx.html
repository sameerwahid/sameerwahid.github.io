Managers' Meetings

The NMRX Managers' Meeting is an informal gathering of NMR facilty managers, taking place on the last day of Reading Week at most Ontario Universities. The morning session often (but not always) includes presentations from NMR or NMR-adjecent companies on current topics of interest, while the afternoon session is an open sharing of ideas, knowledge, and recent experiences.
